Between The Heritage

where the journey of origins, culture, and heritage begins..

HAJI CAFÉ

We begin our tour in Bahrain with a famous traditional breakfast at Haji Café, a popular cafe serving breakfast, lunch, and dinner. It is open from 5:30 a.m. to 9:00 p.m. This restaurant is located in the heart of Manama and provides wonderful local cuisine. They created the restaurant sessions in the typical Bahraini style, with both indoor and outdoor options. Furthermore, the service is quick, and the costs are reasonable.

SOUQ ALMANAMA

Moving on to Manama Souq, which is located next to the haji cafe. It is essentially a tangle of narrow alleyways and passages branching off from Bab Al Bahrain towards the south. Everything from gadgets to cheap t-shirts to herbs may be found here. It is an excellent method to investigate and wander around the bustling streets of a market that retains the atmosphere of an ancient Souq.

TABREEZ RESTAURANT

After exploring Manama Souq, Tabreez Restaurant is the ideal place to acquire a broad range of fresh seafood. The space is massive, with a wide outdoor garden area full of tables and an inside dining room. You can have your fresh seafood prepared anyway you like it: grilled, fried, etc. The flavor is fantastic, and you will not be disappointed. It is open Saturday-Thu 11am-midnight, Friday 12.30pm-midnight on Adhari Avenue in South Sehla.

BAHRAIN FORT MUSEUM & GB CAFE:

The Bahrain Fort museum, is one of Bahrain's most notable attractions, with a history of Bahrain fort dating back to the 14th century AD. It is located in Manama, Bahrain's capital, in the Seef district on the beach, and is open daily from 8:00 am to 6:00 pm. It considers one of the most important historic and architectural sites in the country. After you tour the museum, you can relax with a cup of coffee at the GB cafe outside seats by the sea.
